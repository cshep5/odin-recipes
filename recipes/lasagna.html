Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the best things to bake for the family. It can serve multiple people and even reheats well after sitting in the fridge overnight. Since Lasagna is an italian dish, there are a lot of cheese and tomotoes involved in the recipe.
Ingredients
    Meat
    Garlic
    Onion
    Tomato
    Cheese
    Egg
Steps
    Preheat the oven.
    Mix Ingredients
    Layer into a sheet pan
    Bake at 425 for 25 minutes
    Take out, sprinkle Parmesean cheese and Enjoy!
